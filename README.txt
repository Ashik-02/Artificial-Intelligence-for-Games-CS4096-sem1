How to Run:
- Open the GameBuild folder
- Double-click "Base Guardians - 2d tower defence game.exe"
- The game will start immediately

Controls:
- Left Mouse Button: Place the tower on the available tower slots

Basic Gameplay:
- Enemies automatically spawn and move toward the castle.
- You can place one tower at a time in one of the three dedicated slots.
- The tower automatically attacks enemies in range.
- If enemies reach the castle, they deal 1 point of damage.
- When **4 enemies reach the castle**, the game ends.
